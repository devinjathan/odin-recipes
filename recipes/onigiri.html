Tuna Onigiri

Description

Onigiri is a japanese rice ball that is a great snack or even meal. It can be made with many different fillings, but for this recipe we will be using a tuna mayo base. I find myself taking onigiri's a lot to my university since it is an easy to make meal and very easy to eat on the move.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Canned Tuna
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Salt
  • Rice vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Sriracha optional

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice with preferred method.
  2. While the rice is cooking, we will make the tuna filling. Strain tuna and pour into bowl.
  3. In that same bowl, put kewpie mayo and salt. (if you like spicy tuna, pour sriracha too!) With everything in bowl, mix well.
  4. Once rice is done, pour 1-3 teaspoons of rice vinegar and mix.
  5. With a mold, fill the bottom layer with the cooked rice.
  6. In the middle of the rice layer, put a spoonful of our tuna filling.
  7. Put another layer of rice over and squeeze the mold down.
  8. Remove rice ball and line with seaweed.
  9. Eat fresh, or store with foil or plastic wrap and enjoy!
