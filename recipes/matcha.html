Matcha Latte

Description

The matcha latte is one of my personal favorite drinks that has excellent health benefits along with a delicious taste. This latte has subtle hints of earthiness along with a sweet creamy taste to it. I find this the perfect alternative for a morning cup of coffee. For this recipe, I highly reccomend a ceromonial matcha set for the ideal outcome.

Ingredients

  • Cerominal matcha powder (I reccomend Ippodo-Ikuyo for a more dessert light taste)
  • Hot Water (176Â°F)
  • Milk of your choice
  • Honey (or any other sweetener alternative)
  • *note a matcha set will be used for the recipe*

Steps

  1. Sift matcha into the mixing bowl to remove clumps.
  2. Add hot water to only cover the matcha.
  3. Whisk the matcha in an M motion until a froth layer is formed.
  4. Add honey (or alternate sweetner) into bowl and whisk again.
  5. Pour desired amount of milk into cup then pour matcha on top.
  6. Mix and most importantly enjoy!
