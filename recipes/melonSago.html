Melon Sago

Description

The melon sago is a dessert I found recently that I fell in love with. I like to think of this like a creamy melon cold soup. This dessert is also very easy to make and requires little effort for a delictable treat. If you love honeydew, this is a perfect summer (or whenever!) dessert.

Ingredients

  • Ready to eat Honeydew
  • Sago/Tapioca pearls
  • Nata de coco
  • Coconut pudding
  • Melon milk (I reccomend Binggrae brand!)
  • Evaporated milk
  • Condensed milk

Steps

  1. To start, we are going to boil hot water to start cooking the Sago. To do this we put the desired amount of sago into boiling water and let it cook for 10-20 minutes or until the pearls do not have a white center anymore.
  2. While the sago is cooking, cut the honeydew into bite size pieces and put into a bowl.
  3. Remove the liquids from the Nata de coco and pour the jellies into the bowl.
  4. When the sago is done cooking, run under cold water until the sago is cold and strain and put into our bowl.
  5. Slice or break apart the coconut pudding and put into the bowl.
  6. Pour all the milks into the bowl with the desired amount of each.
  7. Put ice in serving bowl or into the main bowl and enjoy!
